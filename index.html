About "Carbon Clicker"

Carbon Clicker was conceptualized by Ryan Raub & Nathan Rollins of The Julie Ann Wrigley Global Institute of Sustainability, implemented by 3 Computer Science students at Arizona State University during the 2016 - 2017 academic year (Armin Pourdehghan, Luis Carbajal, Lisha Coleman). The vision behind this webgame was to create a fun, interactive way to make students, faculty, and the general public more aware of how to better preserve the environment.

Modeled after the popular webgame "Cookie Clicker", Carbon Clicker is a webgame which aims to teach users about sustainability. Save Earth from being destroyed by collecting carbons and gaining inventory, while learning ways in which you can reduce your carbon footprint.
